About Me

My name is Travis Halleck, and you have landed on my site. I'm a U.S. Army Veteran who served for nine years in various capacities and served honorably in the military. Upon leaving the Army, I wanted to chase my passion of computer programming. After the Army I went to the University of Alabama in Huntsville to earn my bachelor's degree in computer science.
